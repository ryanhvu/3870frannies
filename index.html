About Us

Frannie's Restaurant offers delicious dining and takeout to Hayward, CA. Frannie's Restaurant is a cornerstone in the Hayward community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff. Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
